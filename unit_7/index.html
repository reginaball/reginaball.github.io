About

Regina Ball is a multidisciplinary designer who specializes in graphic design, creative strategy, and branding. Regina's experience ranges from rebranding tech startups to building brands for defense firms, event marketing groups, and more. She graduated magna cum laude with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Graphic Design from Marymount University in Arlington, Virginia. Her specialties are print and web design, branding and identity, advertising and marketing, infographics, typography, motion graphics, and email design.
